About

Tushar Kandpal & Company, Chartered Accountants, is a solution-oriented Proprietorship firm dedicated to delivering excellence through a synergy of knowledge, innovation, and client-centric services. Established with a mission to provide seamless and customized professional support, the firm is managed by a team of dynamic professionals — including Chartered Accountants, Senior Advocates, and Company Secretaries — who bring diversified expertise under one roof.

What sets us apart is our dedication to precision, agility, and practical problem-solving — values that foster long-term trust with clients across industries. Our professionals, having trained with top consulting firms, ensure that every engagement benefits from deep insights, timely execution, and measurable value. At Tushar Kandpal & Company, we don’t just offer services — we offer tailored solutions that help you grow.

Services

Audit & Assurance

We provides audit services as per the requirements under Companies Act, Income Tax Act, Banking Regulation Act, NABARD, etc. We have been providing this service over the last three decades.

Income Tax

We provides Income Tax services such as Tax audit, Transfer Pricing study and reporting, tax consultation, NRI taxation, Scrutiny assessments, appeals, Capital Gain computation and tax advice

Goods & Services Tax (GST)

Goods and Service Tax is a nationwide Indirect taxation system in India. We provide various services under GST namely, registration, audits, consultation on product classification, opinions, obtaining LUT, etc.

Accounting and Payroll

Hiring a full-time accountant and HR personnel is an expensive proposition for small companies. In order to meet their requirements, we provide accounting, payroll and compliance services.

Report and Documents

We have the expertise of preparing reports and documents for various purposes such as seeking Angel/private investments, seed capital, loan from institutions, etc.

NRI Services

We have been rendering Non Resident Indian (NRI) Services in for the last three decades. As per our experience, Non Resident Indians often need the following services, specially tax matters related to sale of property.
